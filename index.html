Main steps of the scientific process:
  1. Make observations
  2. Ask a question
  3. Do background research
  4. Form a hypothesis
  5. Design an experiment to test hypothesis
  6. Analyze data and draw a conclusion
  7. Use results to refine hypothesis, model, or theory
  8. Communicate results
    • Written reports
    • Oral presentations
    • Web pages
